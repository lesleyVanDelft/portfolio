Hello, my name is Lesley.

I am a junior front end developer and I love making beautiful websites.

01. About Me

After working several jobs in a completely different sector, I realized I was not happy with what I was doing. I remembered that in my teenage years I used to make small websites for fun and I really enjoyed doing that. And so I came to the conclusion that I really wanted to become a front end developer. I started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Not long after I felt comfortable with all three languages, I learned to use Sass for CSS, and learned to work with React. So I made the decision to learn to code, specifically front end languages, and I now realize that this is exactly what I want to do.
